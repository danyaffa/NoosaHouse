Location

Noosa House is located in a quiet cul-de-sac on Noosa Hill in Noosa Heads, Queensland. Guests enjoy the peace of a residential street while still being close to Hastings Street, Main Beach and Noosa Junction.

From the house you can easily reach:

  • Noosa Main Beach & Hastings Street
  • Noosa National Park walking tracks
  • Noosa Junction cafés, restaurants, bars and boutiques
  • Alexandria Bay walking trails through the park
  • Sunshine Coast Airport (approx. 30 minutes’ drive)

Exact address details and check-in instructions are provided after booking via Airbnb.
